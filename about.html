About Us

Rimmington’s Pharmacy is a 182-year-old city centre pharmacy situated in the heart of Bradford. It’s the only remaining continuously operating business from the Victorian era still present in the city centre to this day. Founded by Felix Rimmington (personal chemist to queen Victoria), it has recently been awarded a blue plaque by the Bradford Civic Society.

Our COVID-19 journey

Rimmington’s Pharmacy has been a designated site for COVID-19 vaccinations in phases 1 & 2 as part of wave 8. We were part of the first 200 pharmacies nationally to be selected to participate in the vaccination programme, as well as being 1 of only 8 pharmacies in West Yorkshire. We were the only pharmacy in Bradford to be selected to vaccinate cohorts 1-9 as part of the JCVI cohorts and priority groups. With our passion, commitment and experience gained, we have delivered vaccinations across 5 sites to include West Yorkshire (Bradford) and North-East Yorkshire (Grimsby, Goole, Market Weighton & Withernsea).
