Privacy & Policy

This privacy policy notice is for this website; www.rimmingtons.com and served by Rimmingtons pharmacy and governs the privacy of those who use it. The purpose of this policy is to explain to you how we control, process, handle and protect your personal information while browsing or using this website, including your rights under current laws and regulations. If you do not agree to the following policy, you may wish to cease viewing / using this website.

What data is being collected?

The data being collected is your name, email address, phone number, your date of birth, your postal code and your nhs number.

Who is collecting it?

Personal information is collected by Rimmington's pharmacy from you when you book an appointment.

Why are we collecting it?

We require this information in order to give you the vaccine and in order to contact you, when needed. We may send informative emails about new updates or special offers.

How are we using it?

We will use it to confirm who you are when you contact us. We will use it to verify your information by checking your details against our databases.

Do we plan to share it?

We will only share personal information with others when we are legally permitted to do so. We do not share or sell your data to any third parties.

How long will we store it?

We’ll keep your personal information securely stored for as long as we need it.

What control do I have over it?

We will not sell, distribute or lease your personal information to third parties unless we have your permission or are required by law to do so. You may request details of personal information which we hold about you.

Your individual rights

  • the right to be informed
  • the right of access
  • the right to rectification
  • the right to erasure
  • the right to restrict processing
  • the right to data portability
  • the right to object
  • the right not to be subject to automated decision-making including profiling
